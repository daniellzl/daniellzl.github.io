ABOUT ME

I am a lifelong wanderer forever in search of interesting problems to work on. A few years ago, I decided to build a webpage for fun. The a page itself is a simple HTML page dedicated to a personal hero of mine. One thing lead to another and from basic webpages , I moved onto slightly more complicated sites making API calls, then finally towards sophisticated applications using things like React and Node.js.

In search of broader horizons, I began to seek knowledge beyond web development. Instead of simply learning frameworks or technologies from tutorials, I realized that all this technology business is really just representing and transferring information on a computer. So, I taught myself basic computer science data structures and algorithms to understand and optimize the data I was manipulating. Furthermore, building software can be a lonely pursuit, so I sought to contribute to the developer community by joining and eventually leading an algorithms meetup group.

At the current moment, my efforts have lead me to helping the tech start-up Peerbuds build Node.js applications.
